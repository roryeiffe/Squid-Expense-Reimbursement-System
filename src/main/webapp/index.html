About Squid Game

Here at Squid Game, we try to help the poor become rich! We do that by having players play child's game. Here they will compete round after round for the grand prize until there is a one winner.

    Games being played:
  • Ddakji
  • Red Light, Green Light
  • Dalgona/Ppopgi
  • Tug of War
  • Marbles
  • The Glass Tile Game
  • Squid Game
Ddakji

In this game, you attempt to flip the other person's square by throwing it and hitting it on the ground.

Red Light, Green Light

Make it to the finish line first before being caught by the robot.

Dalgona/Ppopgi

In this game, the objective is to get the shape out of the cookie without breaking the shape itself.

Tug of War

In this game, the objective is to pull the flag over to your side before the other team.

Marbles

In this game, the objective is to collect all 20 of the marbles between you and your partner.

Glass Tile Game

In this game, the objective is to get across the glass tile path without falling through the faulty glass.

Squid Game

In this game, the objective of this game is to leave the open house at the top of the figure, and then return back to the house after crossing to the other side.
